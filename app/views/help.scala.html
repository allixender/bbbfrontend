Attendees - Check and Join

  • A headset is strongly recommended to avoid echoing and too much background noise
  • for video sharing, a web cam is required
  • Flash plugin for the browser is required
Plug headset and web cam into your computer BEFORE joining, please!
A user/attendee can join meetings by two means:
  • Being invited with an invitation link from the moderator/session chair
  • Login on the website, list available meetings and join
Join through an invitation link is pretty straightforward:
  • Open the link in the browser (Internet Explorer, Firefox, Chrome ...)
  • Enter your name and click Start
To join via log-in (if you have the website credentials but not the meeting links etc)
  • Open http://localhost:8080/ in your browser
  • Login/meetings ( ask project lead for credentials )
  • You see a list of active meetings
  • Click join to be forwarded to enter your name and join
Within the BigBlueButton video conference console / web meeting application (whatever you want to name it), there are a couple of buttons with some functionality:
  • Share audio (basically join with speech and listening) or mute yourself
  • Share camera (allow other attendees to see you)
The audio (means also your microphone) are already shared! Just wait a moment, and ou will hear others and they should be ablt to hear you!
Your web cam / video is NOT automagically shared, please use the share video button in the upper left corner. This opens a preview window. Now press the play button in that little window.
The moderator has some more privileges:
  • Mute other users as well
  • Share his desktop (let other users see his own computer screen)
  • Upload a presentation (max 2mb pdf as of now ) and click through the presentation

Quick start, create and invite

  • A headset is strongly recommended to avoid echoing and too much background noise
  • for video sharing, a web cam is required
  • Flash plugin for the browser is required
  • Moderator (session chair) opens address in browser: http://localhost:8080/
  • To create a meeting, you must be logged in
  • New Meeting
    • Enter a simple meeting name
    • Assign a moderator password for the meeting (compulsory, min 6 characters and not 'bbb'!)
    • Optionally assign an attendee password (has a default value, if left empty)
    • Check Agree to terms and conditions
If successful, you will be forwarded to the join page
  • Attention: copy the attendee link and send/mail it to your attendees
  • You can copy the moderator link as a backup for yourself (if you have to leave the meeting and want to re-join later with moderator rights)
  • Enter your name and click start
  • You will leave this page and be forwarded to the video conference

FAQ

If something doesn't work, logout, plug-in headset and webcam, and login again.
Be generally patient, due to global audio and video and complex network connections a significant lag can occur, which only means, wait a second longer to respond.
Maybe try a different browser, we have had good and mediocre experiences with Internet Explorer, Mozilla Firefox and Google Chrome.
Make sure, you have access to a good (reliable and reasonable bandwidth) internet connection, you still could switch off your own video sharing and the video dock to ensure at least a proper audio connection.
